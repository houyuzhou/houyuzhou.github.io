Houyu Zhou

I am Houyu Zhou, a PhD candidate in Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong (CityU), under the supervision of Minming Li. I received my BEng from Shandong University (SDU) in June. 2020. I expect to finish my PhD by the beginning of 2024 and will be open to postdoc opportunities.

I am interested in algorithmic mechanism design and computational social choice. I am working on facility location problems for 4 years, and I am also interested in fair allocation and multiwinner approval voting.
