Houyu Zhou

I am Houyu Zhou, a PhD candidate in Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong (CityU), under the supervision of Prof. Minming Li. I received my BEng from Computer Elite Class, Shandong University (SDU) in June 2020. I am visiting John Hopcroft Center for Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, hosted by A/Prof. Biaoshuai Tao. I expect to finish my PhD by June, 2024 and will be open to postdoc opportunities.

I am interested in algorithmic mechanism design and computational social choice, especially facility location games, fair allocation, and multiwinner approval voting.
